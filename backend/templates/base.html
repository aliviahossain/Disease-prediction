📖 Disease Theory

🔵 Diabetic Retinopathy

A diabetes complication affecting the retina's blood vessels. Early detection is critical to prevent blindness.

High Risk if Diabetic

🟢 Glaucoma

Damages the optic nerve due to high eye pressure. One of the leading causes of blindness for people over 60.

Age-Related Risk

🟡 Cataract

Clouding of the eye lens causing blurry vision. The leading cause of blindness worldwide but fully treatable with surgery.

Treatable

🔴 Melanoma

Most serious skin cancer developing in melanocytes. UV exposure increases risk. Early detection is critical.

High Risk

🟠 Psoriasis

Chronic autoimmune condition causing rapid skin cell buildup. Not contagious but requires ongoing management.

Chronic Condition
